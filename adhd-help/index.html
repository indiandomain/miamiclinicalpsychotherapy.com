ADHD HELP FOR CHILDREN IN MIAMI


Does your child face trouble paying attention to any task? Does he have problems in controlling his behavior? Your child could be suffering from Attention Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD). We offer solution to this problem by providing the essential support to children showing clear symptoms of this mental disorder which begins in childhood and may continue through adolescence.

Attention Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) include 3 groups of behavior symptoms: 

Inattention: The child is not able to focus or pay special attention to any activity. He does not listen, gets easily distracted, remain unorganized and loses important things frequently.

Hyperactivity: Being in constant motion (as if “driven by a motor”), fidgeting, or talks too much.

Impulsivity: May demonstrate itself as frequently acting and speaking without thinking, difficulty waiting for things, or interrupting others. 

ADHD is treatable by our team of specialists at MCP. The symptoms need to be studied and analyzed so as to decide the right methodology to follow.

The exact cause of ADHD is not known, but it occurs due to interactions between genes and it often runs in families. ADHD is a complex genetic disorder and the test for diagnosing the disorder is not designed yet. There are certain non-genetic factors too like drinking or smoking during pregnancy, exposure to lead, social deprivation, birth complications and food additives.

MCP Growth Groups offer support for children and teens who are experiencing symptoms of ADHD along with approaches of managing their particular symptoms.
