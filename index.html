PSYCHOTHERAPY GROWTH GROUPS

An approach to engage dynamic therapeutic factors among the group members to facilitate individual personal growth.

This approach will enhance self-awareness, minimize daily stress levels by developing healthy coping skills & promote a positive lifestyle by improving interactions between self & other fellow humans around you.

Discover, Recognize & Experience the difference by allowing yourself to unleash your own positive potential that could emancipate an energy of peace within you, to give & hopefully to receive!

All Psychotherapy Groups are Closed Groups

Each group will have no more than 10 members in order to have more time to focus on each individual.
